In this project, we took a deep dive into the IAB CCPA Compliance Framework to measure end-to-end flows of consent information and understand why opt-out signals are not being honored.

Using data crawled from top websites under different experimental conditions, we examined overall adoption of the framework, the flow of consent information from publishers to third parties, and the reach of opt-out signals. Our results uncovered numerous issues with the adoption and implementation of the framework.

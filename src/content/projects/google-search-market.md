Regulators argue Google Search dominates horizontal web search, while Google claims competition extends to vertical search engines. We presented the first analysis of Google's market share under vertical segmentation.

Leveraging observational trace data from a panel of US residents — including browsing history and Google SERP copies — we found participants' search sessions begin at Google more than 50% of the time in 24 out of 30 vertical market segments.
